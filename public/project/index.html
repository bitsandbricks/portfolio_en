Professional Portfolio

Course: Data Science and Public Policy

I Designed the Data Science and Public Policy Course, an initiative aimed at training employees of the Government of the City of Buenos Aires in the use of new techniques for analysis, evaluation, visualization and communication with data.

Index of Confidence and Access to Vaccines (ICAV)

I helped develop the Index of Confidence and Access to Vaccines (ICAV). The index was designed to identify barriers that hinder access to vaccination, as well as changes in public confidence regarding the importance, safety, and efficacy of vaccines.

Data Science Training for Public Servants

I designed and delivered the Training Program in Data Science for Public Servants at the Argentine’s Government Academy of Public Policy Design.

Supporting Fair Housing with Geospatial Data

I developed a set of geospatial intelligence systems for analyzing and reporting on territorial dynamics related to the location of beneficiaries of public housing programs.
